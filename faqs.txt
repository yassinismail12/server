# ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

## Can foreigners buy property in Egypt?
Yes, foreign buyers can purchase property in Egypt. Some projects may require additional paperwork.

## Do prices include maintenance?
No, prices do not include maintenance fees. These are usually paid on delivery (5–10%).

## Are pets allowed in the compound?
Yes, most compounds are pet-friendly, but some have restrictions on large breeds.

## Do you offer cash discounts?
Yes, some projects offer up to 5% off for cash payments.

## What’s the minimum down payment?
Down payments usually start from 10%, depending on the developer.
